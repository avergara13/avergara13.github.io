Resale Scanner Pro

A mobile-first decision system that turns a thrift-store photo into a structured resale workflow: identify the item, research comparable sales, evaluate margin and risk, prepare a listing, and track the result.

01 · Product workflow

From uncertainty to an informed listing.

The sequence separates observation, assisted work, and human judgment. Each stage produces usable context for the next.

01

Capture

Photograph the item and start a session.

02

Identify

Extract likely brand, model, category, and condition.

03

Research

Review comparable listings and recent sale signals.

04

Decide

Compare cost, fees, margin, and confidence.

05

Prepare

Generate a draft listing and photo checklist.

06

Publish

Move the approved listing into the marketplace.

07

Track

Record sold price and feed real outcomes back into judgment.

03 · Decision design

Useful automation has a stopping rule.

The product is designed around a buy-or-pass decision, not around producing more output. Uncertainty remains visible and the operator stays in control.
