01 · Product workflow

From uncertainty to an informed listing.

The sequence separates observation, assisted work, and human judgment. Each stage produces usable context for the next.

01

Capture

Photograph the item and start a session.

02

Research

Identify the item and review comparable market signals.

03

Decide

Compare cost, fees, margin, risk, and confidence.

04

Prepare

Create and approve the listing before publication.

05

Track

Record outcomes and feed evidence back into judgment.

03 · Decision design

Useful automation has a stopping rule.

The product is designed around a buy-or-pass decision, not around producing more output. Uncertainty remains visible and the operator stays in control.
